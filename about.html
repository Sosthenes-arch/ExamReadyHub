About ExamReadyHub

ExamReadyHub is a free exam preparation platform covering 150+ multiple-choice tests across driving, IT certifications, academic admissions, medical licensing, law, finance, government, teaching, and more — all in one place, all for free.

Why ExamReadyHub?

Most exam prep resources are either paywalled, cluttered with ads, or buried in app stores. ExamReadyHub is built around a simple belief: everyone deserves access to quality practice questions without a subscription fee or account creation.

Every question includes an instant explanation so you understand why the answer is correct — not just what it is. That's what turns practice into genuine preparation.

How it works

Select any exam from the homepage, answer one question at a time, and get immediate feedback after each answer. At the end, you'll see your score, a breakdown of every question, and the option to retry. No login, no email, no payment — just practice.

Global coverage

ExamReadyHub covers exams from around the world — from the US DMV and CompTIA to the UK A-Levels and PLAB, India's NEET and UPSC, South Korea's Suneung, Japan's Common Test, Germany's Abitur, and dozens more. Whether you're sitting a local driving test or a global professional certification, you'll find practice material here.

Accuracy & Disclaimer

ExamReadyHub practice questions are designed to reflect the style and content of real exams based on publicly available study guides and syllabi. However, ExamReadyHub is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to any testing organisation (including but not limited to the DMV, CompTIA, AWS, College Board, USMLE, FINRA, PMI, UPSC, or any other body listed on this site). Always refer to official study materials as your primary resource.

Growing question banks

Each exam currently has a starter set of practice questions. We are continuously expanding every question bank. If you notice an error in a question or explanation, or would like to suggest a new exam, please let us know via the contact page.
